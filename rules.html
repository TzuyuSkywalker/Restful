Official Guild Rules

Assistance

Attending the castle siege is an important requirement, if you are not free on Sundays to attend then you do not have space in our guild. Only the 8 players with the highest attendance in weekly guild point contributions will be allowed to participate in the Archangel Box, as long as they have at least their current tier weapons unlocked AT LEAST in "6E" prior to this you do not need this weapon, what you need is to use garnets to level up your equipment.

Loot Distribution

Loot will ALWAYS be fair AS LONG AS your progress doesn't stall. By this, we mean that if your CP and level are LOW or you don't meet the requirements, you can't claim anything, since everyone else is playing daily and upgrading their gear, while you want everything for free because you think life owes you a favor for being born or that you think you deserve more for having invested money when no one forced you to do so.

Gear & Progression

Never stop progressing. Don't save up garnets thinking you'll be able to roll something for free. Each item will have a specific roll based on your level/power/character class. We don't care if you build hybrid. Set gear will prioritize gear that corresponds to each class. Even if you're a "DK STR BUILDING HYBRID," you won't have priority on ENE gear, for example.

Community & Respect

I don't want a single fucking drama queen in our guild, if something doesn't belong to you or you did something wrong, shut up, there's a button that says "leave guild" we don't need to get bitter, we come to the game to play and have fun as a team, here nobody is better than anyone else, the only ones who are better than someone are those who are committed to helping the members.
